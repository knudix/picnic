FAQ:

  • Q: Why does the app show the same country for both sides of the converter?
  • A: The app uses your location as well as phones region. The region can be changed under Settings>General>Language and Region
  • Q: I want to select another country than the ones proposed!
  • A: It is possible to override countries by adding country-identifiers under System Preferences > Picnic. These must be of the format xx_XX i.e: en_US. Better integration for overriding will come in a future release.
